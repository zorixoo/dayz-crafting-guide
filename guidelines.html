Communication Guidelines

To keep our community welcoming and respectful for everyone, please follow these basic rules when posting comments or interacting on this site.

What is Not Allowed:

  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, or any kind of hate speech
  • Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • Threats of violence or illegal activities
  • Spam, advertising, or unrelated links
  • Offensive or explicit language or content
  • Sharing personal information of others

What We Encourage:

  • Respectful and constructive comments
  • Helpful feedback and suggestions
  • Positive and friendly communication
  • Sharing knowledge and resources related to DayZ

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in comment removal or banning from the community.
