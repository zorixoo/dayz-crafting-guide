Base Building Guide for Starters

Step 1: Craft a Fence Kit

Combine 2 Short Sticks and 1 Rope to create a Fence Kit.

Step 2: Place the Fence Kit & Add Logs

Place the Fence Kit on flat ground. Attach 2 Wooden Logs and use a Shovel or Pickaxe to build support posts.

Step 3: Build the Frames

Attach 18–24 Planks and 40–60 Nails to form the fence frame. Use a Hammer, Hatchet, or Sledgehammer to build the lower and upper frames.

Step 4: Add the Gate Mechanism

Attach 1 Metal Wire to the center. Equip Pliers and select Build Gate to finalize the gate.

Optional Step: Secure the Gate

You can add a Combination Lock to secure your gate.

Notes

  • The gate opens from only one side.
  • Frames must be fully built before adding the gate mechanism.
  • You can add barbed wire or camouflage netting after building the gate.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Placing fence kits on uneven or sloped ground — this causes build errors and instability.
  • Trying to add the gate mechanism before building both the lower and upper fence frames.
  • Not having enough nails or planks before starting — gather more to avoid interruption.
  • Forgetting to attach support posts (using shovel or pickaxe) — these keep your fence stable.
  • Leaving gates unlocked or unprotected — always add a combination lock if possible.
  • Building too close to other player bases — might cause conflict or block your own expansions.

How Do I Repair My Fence Gate?

Use a Hammer or Hatchet with nails to repair damaged fence parts. Regularly check for damage after fights or bad weather.

Can I Move or Dismantle a Built Fence?

Once built, fences cannot be picked up or moved. You will need to dismantle parts individually or build a new fence elsewhere.

What Materials Are Best for Base Defense?

Wooden fences provide basic protection, but combining them with barbed wire and camouflage netting greatly improves defense. Metal fences offer stronger resistance but require more resources.

How Do I Upgrade My Base Over Time?

Start with basic fences and gates, then add reinforcements like metal panels, traps, and locks. Keep upgrading materials and adding layers for better security.

How Do Fences Work in Multiplayer?

Friends and trusted players can open your gates if you share the combination lock code. Enemies cannot open locked gates, so always secure your base.

What Common Traps or PvP Tactics Should I Watch Out For?

Beware of enemy players sneaking through unlocked gates or placing explosives near your base. Use watchpoints and keep your base well-lit and guarded.

How to Camouflage Your Base Effectively?

Use natural foliage, camouflage netting, and build near trees or terrain features to blend your base into the environment and avoid detection.

How Do Weather and Decay Affect Base Building?

Weather can damage your fence over time. Regularly repair and maintain your base to prevent decay and keep it strong against the elements.
